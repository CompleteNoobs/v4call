v4call — Rate Editor

Create your communication rate schedule and post it to Hive
YOUR HIVE ACCOUNT
Your rate schedule will be posted to your Hive blog as a post titled v4call-rates. The v4call app reads the latest post with this title to determine your call rates. Posting requires Hive Keychain browser extension.
RATE LISTS
Create named lists of users with custom rates. Anyone not in a list uses the default rates. Lists are checked in order — first match wins.
OUTPUT
Posting to Hive requires the Hive Keychain browser extension. The post will be titled v4call-rates and tagged v4call. The app always reads your most recent post with this title.
RATE REFERENCE
RING — fee to make your phone ring
CONNECT — fee when you accept the call
RATE — per-hour cost, prorated per minute
TEXT — flat fee to send you a message
FREE — enter 0 for any field
All amounts in HBD (≈ USD)
Times are in your local timezone
Duration billing prorated per minute
